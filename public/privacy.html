Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Postcode Revealer requests temporary access to your device location to determine the nearest postcode. We strive to minimize the amount of data collected, stored, or shared while providing this service.

What we collect

When you tap “Use my location,” the app receives your latitude and longitude directly from the browser’s Geolocation API. This information is used once to request reverse-geocoding results from OpenStreetMap’s Nominatim service.

How data is used

  • Coordinates are transmitted over HTTPS to Nominatim solely to retrieve the postcode and human-readable address.
  • The returned address details are displayed locally in your browser and are not stored on our servers.

Data retention

We do not persist, log, or sell your location or address information. The data lives only in your session memory until you refresh the page or close the tab.

Third-party services

Reverse geocoding is powered by OpenStreetMap’s Nominatim API, which is subject to its own privacy policy. Your request includes your browser’s IP address and the coordinates needed to complete the lookup.

Your choices

  • You can decline the location permission at any time.
  • You can revoke the permission from your browser settings or clear your site data to reset the prompt.
